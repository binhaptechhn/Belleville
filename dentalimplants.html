Dental Implant

Dental Implant is a technique to restore a lost tooth by implanting an Implant post into the jaw bone to make a false tooth root.
Currently, Bel Dental is one of the TOP units applying the most prestigious and quality-guaranteed implant technology in the market. Coming to us, you will be able to use professional services at extremely competitive and preferential prices, ensuring absolute satisfaction for you.

Appointment

step-by-step process

Step 1

Taking Panorama film is an indispensable step in the initial examination stage. Based on the number of missing teeth, the health of the teeth, the condition of the jawbone, the position to be implanted, the doctor will discuss with the customer to come up with the most suitable route and briefly talk about the advanced method of tooth transplantation. Advance Implant.

Step 2

Then, the missing tooth position is opened and the appropriate size titanium post is slowly placed into the position of the jawbone. This stage is completed, the doctor will close the stitch so that most customers do not feel any discomfort at all.

Step 3

Usually, customers who perform Implant placement need to return for a follow-up examination as directed by a specialist. Especially 7 days after surgery is the most important milestone. At this time, the doctor will remove the sutures and check the progress of the surgery.

Step 4

When ensuring that the jawbone and the Implant post have achieved perfect compatibility, the doctor will place the healing post. Next, the client proceeds to take impressions of the molars . This porcelain crown is designed to look like the real thing to achieve the expected aesthetic.

Step 5

The last step plays an important role, that is, the doctor will carry out the extraction of gum tissue and attach the Abutment joint to the position of the Implant post with a screw. Abutment has the role of supporting new teeth, ensuring aesthetic function and chewing as usual.
